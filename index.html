MY SUNDAY BEST CHICKEN CURRY PLANTAIN SAUCE

RECIPE INFORMATION.

  • Preparation Time: 30-45mins
  • Cooking time: 30mins
  • Servings: 4 small bowls
  • Difficulty: Beginner

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 Medium finger sized plantain
  • 500g of diced chicken breast
  • 3 cups of chicken stock
  • 5 large diced carrots
  • 1/2 cup of red bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup of green bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup of yellow bell pepper
  • 1 whole diced onions
  • 1 whole chopped fresh tomatoes
  • curry masala
  • rosemary
  • thyme
  • black pepper
  • 1/2 cup of ginger and garlic
  • 1/2 cup of olive oil
  • 1/2 cup of maggi cubes
  • Salt
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce
  • 300g of magerine

INSTRUCTIONS..

  1. Peel and slice the plantains into small pieces.
  2. In a pot, heat the olive oil and saut√© the diced onions until translucent.
  3. Add the diced chicken breast and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in the ginger and garlic, cooking for an additional 2 minutes.
  5. Add the chopped tomatoes, bell peppers, and carrots, cooking until softened.
  6. Season with curry masala, rosemary, thyme, black pepper, and salt to taste.
  7. Pour in the chicken stock and bring to a boil.
  8. Reduce heat and let it simmer for about 10 minutes.
  9. Add the sliced plantains and let simmer until they are tender.
  10. Adjust seasoning with soy sauce and maggi cubes as needed.
  11. Serve hot in bowls, garnished with fresh herbs if desired.
  12. Enjoy your delicious chicken curry plantain porridge!

TIPS.

  • Use ripe plantains for a sweeter taste.
  • Adjust the spice level by adding more or less curry masala.
  • For a creamier texture, add coconut milk during cooking.
  • Serve with a side of steamed vegetables for added nutrition.
  • Store leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days.
  • Reheat gently on the stove or in the microwave before serving.
  • Feel free to experiment with different vegetables based on your preference.
  • Pair with a side of crusty bread or rice for a complete meal.
  • Enjoy the dish with family and friends for a delightful dining experience.
